About Kak Da Kwaychap Mee Celup

Kak Da Kwaychap Mee Celup is a family-run business since 2016, serving fresh, flavorful Mee Celup using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

1980s Bunut Payong, Kota Bharu Kak Da opened a humble coconut-leaf stall. She cooked Kelantan-style broth from scratch to feed her neighbours and sells it at a low price to locals. Today, her daughter upholds the legacy. There are no shortcuts. It is just the same hearty, home-cooked flavour that has been warming community bellies for over 40 years.

The majority of the customers are locals, but there are also many tourists who want to try Kak Da Kwaychap  Mee Celup due to recommendations from social media or friends.

Our Mee Celup features a rich and flavorful broth with the perfect blend of Thai and local Kelantanese tastes, available only in Kelantan.

Kak Da Kwaychap Mee Celup wants to grow the business in the future by opening additional locations in other states. Additionally, in order bring in more clients and advertise the brand on social media, the company intends to launch new menu items. beside that, Kak Da Kwaychap Mee Celup wants to become one of Malaysia's most well-known mee celup brands and enhance customer service.

Our mission is to provide authentic taste with high-quality ingredients and excellent customer service.
